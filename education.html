What I Learned in Information Management Courses

    Focuses on the systematic control of records throughout their life cycle, including creation, storage, use, and disposal.
  2. Cataloging
    Involves organizing and describing information resources using standard rules to support easy retrieval and access.
  3. Electronic Publishing
    Introduces the process of producing and designing digital content by using Canva.
  4. Multimedia for Information Presentation
    Explores the use of multimedia elements such as images, audio, video, and graphics to present information effectively by using Adobe Premiere Pro
  5. Web Content Management and Design
    Focuses on creating, managing, and organizing web content using basic HTML and design styles.
  6. Foundation of Archives
    Provides an understanding of archival preservation practices on important materials.
